# Questions for Annex II

- **Manufacturer identification**: Have you provided the **manufacturer’s name, registered trade name or trademark, postal address, email or digital contact**, and, where available, the **website** where users can reach you?
- **Single Point of Contact (SPOC)**: Have you included a **single point of contact** for reporting and receiving information about **vulnerabilities**, along with a reference to your **coordinated vulnerability disclosure (CVD) policy**?
- **Product Identification**: Does your documentation specify the **name, type, and any additional details** that allow **unique identification** of the product with digital elements?
- **Intended Purpose and Security Environment**: Have you clearly described the **intended purpose**, **essential functionalities**, and the **security environment** of the product as provided by the manufacturer?
	-  ‘Intended Purpose’ means the use for which a product with digital elements is intended by the manufacturer, including the specific context and conditions of use, as specified in the information supplied by the manufacturer in the instructions for use, promotional or sales materials and statements, as well as in the technical documentation
- **Foreseeable Misuse and Associated Risks**: Have you informed users about any **known or reasonably foreseeable circumstances or misuse** that may lead to **significant cybersecurity risks**?
- **EU Declaration of Conformity Access**: Where applicable, have you provided the **internet address** where the **EU Declaration of Conformity** for the product can be accessed?
- **Technical Security Support Information**: Have you described the **type of technical security support** offered by the manufacturer and specified the **end-date of the support period** for receiving security updates?
- **Detailed User Instructions**: Have you provided **detailed security-related instructions** (or a link to them) covering the following aspects:
	- Measures for **secure installation, commissioning, and operation** throughout the product’s lifetime;
	- How **changes to the product** could affect **data security**;
	- How to **install security updates**;
	- How to **decommission or dispose** of the product securely, ensuring data removal;
	- How to **disable automatic installation of security updates** if desired; ( Annex I Part I point C )
	- For integrators, how to meet **Annex I cybersecurity requirements**  and **Annex VII documentation requirements** (these are covered above) when the product is integrated into other systems.
- **Software Bill of Materials (SBOM)**: If a **software bill of materials (SBOM)** is made available, have you informed users **how and where** it can be accessed?
