# Questions - Design, Development & Cybersecurity Risk Management - Article 13 
## **Design and Compliance**
- Has the product with digital elements (PDE) been **designed, developed, and produced** in compliance with the **essential cybersecurity requirements** outlined in Annex I, Part I?
## **Cybersecurity Risk Assessment**
- Have you **assessed cybersecurity risks** across all lifecycle phases (from design to maintenance)?
-  Does this assessment ensure that risks are **minimized and incidents are prevented**?
## **Risk Assessment Documentation**
-  Is the **cybersecurity risk assessment documented and regularly updated** throughout the support period?
- Does it include intended and foreseeable use, operational conditions, and expected duration?
- Does it describe how **Annex I cybersecurity and vulnerability-handling measures** are implemented?
- Have you provided a **clear justification** for any cybersecurity requirements deemed not applicable?
## **Inclusion in Technical Documentation**
- Is the **risk assessment included** in the **technical documentation**, as required by Article 31 and Annex VII?
## **Integration of Third-Party Components** 
- Do you exercise **due diligence** when integrating third-party or open-source components to ensure they do not compromise cybersecurity?
## **Vulnerability Reporting & Cooperation**
- When vulnerabilities are found in components, do you **report them to the component maintainer** or responsible party?
-  Do you **remediate** vulnerabilities appropriately and **share fixes or code** with maintainers when relevant?
## **Systematic Documentation**
- Do you **systematically document** all cybersecurity aspects, including discovered vulnerabilities, risk analyses, and updates?
- Is this documentation **proportionate** to the product’s risk level and kept current?
## **Vulnerability Handling & Support Period**
- Are vulnerabilities **handled effectively throughout the support period**?
- Have you determined and justified the **support period duration** (minimum 5 years)?
- Do you maintain formal **policies and procedures**, including a **Coordinated Vulnerability Disclosure (CVD)** policy?
## **Availability of Security Updates**
- Are all **security updates** made available for **at least 10 years** or for the full support period, whichever is longer?
## **Substantially Modified Software Versions**
- If a substantially modified software version is released, do you ensure previous users can **upgrade to the latest version** free of charge and without additional compatibility costs?
## Public Software Archives
- If you maintain **public archives of older software versions**, do you **clearly inform users** of the **security risks** of using unsupported versions?
## **Conformity Assessment & CE Marking**
- Before placing the product on the market, have you completed all steps of **technical documentation, conformity assessment**, issued an **EU Declaration of Conformity**, and **affixed the CE marking**?
## **Retention of Documentation**
- Do you **retain all technical documentation and EU Declarations of Conformity** for at least **10 years** or the entire **support period**, whichever is longer?
## **Ongoing Compliance for Series Production**
- Do you maintain **ongoing conformity** for series-produced products, accounting for changes in design, production, or standards?
## **Product Identification**
- Does each product carry a **type, batch, or serial number**, or equivalent traceable identifier on the product, its packaging, or accompanying documents?
## **Manufacturer Contact Details**
- Have you provided the **manufacturer’s name, trade name, postal address, digital contact information, and website** (if available) on the product, packaging, or documentation?
- Are these details presented in a **language easily understood** by both users and authorities?
## **Single Point of Contact (SPOC)**
- Have you designated a **Single Point of Contact (SPOC)** for users to report vulnerabilities and communicate directly?
- Is the SPOC **clearly identifiable**, supports **multiple communication channels**, and does **not rely solely on automation**?
## **User Information & Instructions (Annex II)**
- Have you provided **user information and instructions** (Annex II) in **paper or electronic form**, written in a **clear, understandable language**?
- Do these instructions enable **secure installation, operation, and use** of the product?
- Are they **accessible and retained for at least 10 years** (or for the support period), even when hosted online?
## **Disclosure of Support Period**
- Is the **end date of the support period** (month and year) **clearly communicated at the time of purchase**?
- Where feasible, is this date also displayed on the **product, packaging, or digitally**?
- Do users receive a **notification** when the product’s support period ends (if technically feasible)?
## **EU Declaration of Conformity**
- Does each product include either a **full EU Declaration of Conformity** or a **simplified version** that provides a **direct internet link** to the full declaration?
## **Corrective Measures for Non-Conformity**
- If you **know or suspect** a product or its processes no longer meet CRA requirements, do you **immediately correct**, **withdraw**, or **recall** the affected product?
## **Cooperation with Market Authorities**
- Upon a **reasoned request**, can you provide **all documentation and information** (in an understandable language) to demonstrate conformity?
- Do you **cooperate fully with authorities** to remove or mitigate any identified cybersecurity risks?
## **Cessation of Operations**
- If you plan to **cease operations**, do you **inform authorities and users in advance**, explaining the cessation and its effects on product support?
## **Software Bill of Materials (SBOM) Format**
- Are you prepared to provide a **Software Bill of Materials (SBOM)** that follows the **standard format and structure** defined by the European Commission?
## **Union-Wide Dependency Assessment**
- If requested by **ADCO or authorities**, can you provide **SBOMs** for inclusion in a Union-wide dependency assessment?
- Are you aware that only **anonymized and aggregated SBOM data** will be shared by authorities with the Cooperation Group under **NIS2**?
