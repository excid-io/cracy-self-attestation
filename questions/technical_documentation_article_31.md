# Article 31 - Technical Documentation 

-  **Completeness and Required Content**: Does your technical documentation include all relevant data and details demonstrating that:
    - the product with digital elements complies with the **essential cybersecurity requirements** in Annex I (check questions in the Annex I) , and
    - the manufacturer’s processes also comply with those requirements?
	- Does your technical documentation include **all required elements** listed in **Annex VII** (check questions for Annex VII) ?
-  **Preparation Before Market Placement**: Was the technical documentation **prepared before** the product with digital elements was placed on the market?
- **Updating During the Support Period**: Do you have a process to **continuously update** the technical documentation, where appropriate, **throughout the entire support period**?****
-  **Products Covered by Multiple EU Legal Acts**: If your product falls under **multiple EU legal acts** requiring technical documentation (as per Article 12) (I don't understand this):
    - Have you prepared a **single set** of technical documentation that includes:
        - all required information from **Annex VII**, and
        - all required information from the other applicable Union legal acts?
-  **Language Requirements**: Is the technical documentation—and any correspondence related to conformity assessment—available in:
    - the **official language** of the Member State where the notified body is established, **or**
    - another language acceptable to that notified body?
- **Future Delegated Acts** : Are you prepared to comply with future **delegated acts** that may add new required elements to the technical documentation in Annex VII?
	- Do you have the capability to integrate updates related to:
	    - technological developments, and
	    - evolving implementation practices under this Regulation?
-  **Proportionality and Administrative Burden**: Are you aware that the Commission must ensure proportionality for microenterprises and SMEs, and do you monitor Commission updates that may affect documentation obligations?
