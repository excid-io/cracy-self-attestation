# Questions - Voluntary Reporting - Article 15
- **Voluntary Vulnerability Reporting**: Do you understand that you can voluntarily report vulnerabilities or cyber threats affecting the risk profile of your product with digital elements to a CSIRT coordinator or ENISA? (probably yes or no question)
- **Voluntary Incident Reporting**: Do you understand that you have the ability to voluntarily notify a CSIRT coordinator or ENISA about security incidents or near misses that could have resulted in an incident? ( probably yes or no question )
- **Processing of Voluntary Reports**: Are you aware that voluntary notifications are processed by the CSIRT coordinator or ENISA according to the same procedure as mandatory notifications under Article 16? (will be analyzed in another)
- **Prioritisation of Mandatory Reports**: Do you understand that CSIRTs may prioritise mandatory notifications over voluntary ones?
- **Third-Party Notification Handling**: If a third party (not you, the manufacturer) reports an actively exploited vulnerability or severe incident related to your product, are you aware that the CSIRT coordinator must inform you **without undue delay**?
- **Confidentiality Protections**: Do you understand that CSIRTs and ENISA must protect the confidentiality of information submitted through voluntary reporting?
- **No Additional Obligations for Voluntary Reporting**: Are you aware that voluntary reporting does **not** impose any additional legal obligations or penalties on the reporting person beyond what would already apply?
